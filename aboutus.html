Our story

Founded in 2014 on the principle that time is our most precious asset, Velocity deploys cutting edge technologies and human experts to transform how high performance people discover and enjoy their world. We help our members do and be more in the digital age. ‍ Backed by $28 million in venture capital, we are one of the world’s fastest growing companies according to the Financial Times, and have delivered for members in over 60 countries.

“Combining human expertise with artificial intelligence Velocity Black is a lifestyle assistant that lives on your phone. As well as the perks – from complimentary upgrades to preferred rates – the instant messenger service allows customers to tailor-make their lives, whether you need help at Burning Man or tickets to the Wimbledon final – all at the press of a button, and within seconds.”
